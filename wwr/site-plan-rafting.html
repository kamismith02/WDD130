Overview

Purpose

This website provides easier access to our water rafting business for our customers to see what we have to offer for them!

Audience

Who are the target customers? Anyone interested in water activities and being outdoors, lots to zero experience required. What do they want? To spend time outside and learn how to balance to ups and downs of the river. What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website? Our customers will find information on rafting tours, renting rafts, and any rafting info needs.
